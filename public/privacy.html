Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Tilapiya Restaurant ("we", "us") operates an automated customer-service assistant on WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and Instagram. This policy explains what information we collect through those channels and how we use it.

Information we collect

When you message us, we receive your name (as shown on the messaging platform), your phone number or platform user ID, and the contents of your messages, including any images or voice notes you send.

How we use it

We use this information solely to respond to your inquiries, manage reservations and orders, send booking reminders and follow-ups you have requested, and improve our service. Messages may be processed by an AI assistant to generate replies. Our staff can also view conversations to assist you personally.

Storage and sharing

Conversation data is stored securely in our customer-relationship system. We do not sell your personal information or share it with third parties for marketing. Data is shared only with the service providers needed to operate the system (messaging delivery via Meta Platforms, secure database hosting, and AI processing), each bound to use it only to provide the service.

Your choices

You can stop messaging us at any time. To request deletion of your conversation history and personal data, message us "DELETE MY DATA" on the same channel or contact us using the details below, and we will remove it within 30 days.

Contact

Tilapiya Restaurant — for any privacy questions, contact us via WhatsApp on our business number or by email.
